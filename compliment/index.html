complement;
noun

a thing that contributes extra features to something else in such a way as to improve or emphasize its quality. "local ales provide the perfect complement to fine food"

verb

contribute extra features to (someone or something) in such a way as to improve or emphasize their qualities. "a classic blazer complements a look that's smart or casual"
